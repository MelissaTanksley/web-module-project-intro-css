Yosemite National Park


Come explore California's famouse National park!

Always open. Reservations available by phone and online.

Not just a great valley, but the shrine to human foresight, I don't know what this says, power of glaciers,
the persistence of life, and the tranquility of nature.

Fire is a natural and essential part of Yosemite. We manage fire carefully and study how it interacts with the park's ecosystem.
This blog provides updates about fires in Yosemite. Check for current fire restrictions.

We strongly recommend that you make reservations for lodging, camping, and backpacking.
You can pay the park entrance fee upon arrival (there is no need to pay it in advance).
